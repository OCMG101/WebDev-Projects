EVENTS

#SOLFEST23

SolFest 2023 was undoubtedly the holy grail of concerts in Kenya for the year 2023. Pulling an enormous crowd of 15,000 fans, it was a worthy celebration of Sauti Sol's remarkable 20-year music journey before taking an indefinite hiatus. The event featured a VIP show on 2nd November and a Fan show on 4th November.

Sauti Sol headlined the event and they featured guest artists from the vibrant Kenyan music scene, including Nyashinski, Kaligraph Jones, Mordecai Dex, Bensoul, Nviiri, Dela, P-Unit, and Lisa Oduor Noah.

The concert was a massive success, with both the crowd and performers having the time of their lives. The energy was electric, and the atmosphere was filled with excitement and joy. It was a fitting tribute to Sauti Sol's two decades of music excellence, and the audience reveled in the celebration of their iconic journey. Sauti Sol took the audience on one hell of an emotional rollercoaster with performances of their remarkable music catalogue from 'Lazizi' all the way to 'Lil Mama'.

CLASSICS IN NAIROBI

The 'Classics in Nairobi' concert by the Kenya Conservatoire of Music Symphony Orchestra on July 15, 2023, at the Kenya National Theatre was a captivating musical experience. The orchestra showcased a diverse repertoire, starting with the powerful Beethoven Symphony No. 5, followed by the delightful Light Cavalry Overture and the majestic Handel's Hallelujah Chorus. Adding a local touch, the concert featured a Kenyan classic medley, seamlessly blending Safari ya Bamba and Nimerudi Shambani which also featured the Kenya Conservatoire of Music Choir.

The performance also incorporated unique elements, such as a brass ensemble rendition of the 'Fairy Odd Parents' theme song, adding a playful and nostalgic atmosphere. Furthermore, a saxophone quartet took the stage to enchant the audience with their interpretation of Stevie Wonder's timeless hit 'Sir Duke'. The concert was a harmonious blend of classical masterpieces and contemporary tunes, showcasing the versatility and talent of the Kenya Conservatoire of Music Symphony Orchestra.
